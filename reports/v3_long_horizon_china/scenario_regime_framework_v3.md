## Interpretation
- The final subset is intentionally narrow: two regime-narrative questions, one governance question, and one allocation-tilt question.
- The house-view ladder and 10% calibration questions remain useful internal checks, but they are too trivial for the main deck because the benchmark already sits above those levels at these anchors.
- The US and EM tilt questions are secondary. US is directionally coherent but too small for a headline slide; EM did not move materially in this batch.
- China remains fully in the system, but not in the main-deck subset.
